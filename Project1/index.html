How To start a business 101.

This site will tell you how to start a lash company step by step.

  1. Create a name.
  2. Find a vendor.
  3. Pick a platform to sale from.
  4. Open Business.

Step 1 creating a name.

When creating a name make sure the name isn't taken. Once you find a name you like trademark or copyright your brand. This is a very important step when creating a business.

Step 2 Finding a vendor.

There's alot of ways to find a Lash vendor unfortunately I will be explaining two ways. The first way you can find a lash vendor would be Youtube. Yes I said Youtube, Many people learn things from watching Youtube videos. I strongly reccommend.

Step 3 Pick a platform to sale from.

Decide where you would like to sale your lashes. It can be on social media or in person. Once you decide your platform you should be ready for step 5.

Step 5 Open your Business.

Once you successfully completed steps 1-4 you are ready to start business. This step is where the promoting comes in. You can promote your business any way you would like. Rather its business cards, or social media ads.

Remember Never give up on your dreams and Always keep faith!
